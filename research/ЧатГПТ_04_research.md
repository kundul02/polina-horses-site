# Executive Summary  
We identified several global opportunities for youth (child/teen) horse riders that meet the criteria of paid travel, accommodation, training, free programs, or paid projects. These include paid casting calls for children with riding skills, free or funded equestrian training programs, international exchange teams through youth pony clubs, and supervised riding camps with lodging. Each opportunity offers at least one qualifying benefit (e.g. free attendance, covered lodging, paid role). The top matches (see Table below) include:  

- **Paid Casting** – A UK production casting call for a 5–9 year-old male rider, offering a paid role (competitive rate).  
- **Funded Training Camp (UK)** – The *Riding A Dream Academy* offers free residential riding weeks in Newmarket for 14–16 year-olds (boarding and daily instruction provided).  
- **International Pony Club Exchanges (USA & Abroad)** – US Pony Club teams (ages ~16–25) travel internationally (Canada, UK, Australia, etc.) with travel and host-family lodging provided.  
- **Overnight Riding Camp (Australia)** – Glenworth Valley’s school-holiday horse camps (ages 5–16) include multi-day riding with lodging and meals provided (paid program, but with training and accommodation).
